# CareLog Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

CareLog ("we," "our," "us," or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, web portal, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

This policy complies with:
- Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023 (India)
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) (United States)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name and contact information (email, phone number)
- Account credentials
- Profile information (age, gender)
- Emergency contact details

### 2.2 Health Information
- Vital signs measurements (blood pressure, glucose, temperature, weight, pulse, SpO2)
- Medical documents (prescriptions, lab reports)
- Medical photographs (wounds, symptoms)
- Voice and video recordings related to health
- Care plan information
- Medical conditions and diagnoses

### 2.3 Technical Information
- Device identifiers and type
- Operating system version
- App usage analytics
- Error logs and crash reports (sanitized of personal information)
- Network connectivity status

### 2.4 Location Information
- We do not collect precise location data
- Approximate location may be inferred from IP address for security purposes

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Delivery
- Tracking and displaying your health vitals
- Enabling data sharing with authorized caregivers and healthcare providers
- Generating alerts based on configured thresholds
- Facilitating communication within your care team

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyzing anonymized usage patterns to improve app functionality
- Identifying and fixing technical issues
- Developing new features based on user needs

### 3.3 Communication
- Sending health-related notifications and alerts
- Providing customer support
- Sending service announcements and updates

### 3.4 Legal and Compliance
- Complying with legal obligations
- Responding to legal process
- Protecting our rights and the safety of users

## 4. Information Sharing

### 4.1 With Your Care Team
We share your health information only with individuals you explicitly authorize:
- Family members and caregivers you invite
- Healthcare providers you grant access to
- Attendants authorized by your caregivers

### 4.2 Service Providers
We may share information with third-party service providers who assist in operating our Services:
- Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS)
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
- Customer support tools

All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law, such as:
- Court orders or legal process
- Government agency requests
- Protection of public safety

### 4.4 We Never Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage Location
- Data for users in India is stored in AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) region
- Data for users in other regions is stored in appropriate regional data centers
- All data remains within the specified geographic boundaries

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement comprehensive security measures:
- Encryption at rest using AES-256
- Encryption in transit using TLS 1.2+
- Certificate pinning in mobile applications
- Multi-factor authentication support
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- 24/7 security monitoring

### 5.3 Access Controls
- Role-based access controls limit data access to authorized personnel
- All data access is logged and auditable
- Employees undergo background checks and security training

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts
We retain your data for the duration of your account plus 7 years for legal and compliance purposes.

### 6.2 Health Records
Health records are retained as required by applicable medical record retention laws, which may require longer retention periods.

### 6.3 Audit Logs
Security and access audit logs are retained for a minimum of 7 years.

### 6.4 Deleted Accounts
Upon account deletion:
- Personal identifiers are removed immediately
- Health records are anonymized but retained per legal requirements
- Uploaded files are permanently deleted

## 7. Your Rights

Under DPDP 2023 and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 7.2 Right to Correction
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 7.3 Right to Erasure
You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability
You can export your health data in a standard format (FHIR JSON).

### 7.5 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

### 7.6 Right to Grievance Redressal
You can file complaints about data handling practices.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@carelog.health or use the in-app settings.

## 8. Children's Privacy

CareLog is designed for adult users. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental consent. If we learn we have collected information from a child without consent, we will delete it promptly.

## 9. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes through:
- In-app notifications
- Email to registered users
- Posted notice on our website

Your continued use of the Services after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
